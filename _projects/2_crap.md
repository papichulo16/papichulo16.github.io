Automatically scans binaries for common vulnerabilities and exploit them. Some of these exploits include ret2system, ret2libc, ROP write primitives, printf GOT overwrite, etc. Solved 120/120 binaries in 58 seconds, breaking the previous record at 170 seconds.

[source](https://www.github.com/papichulo16/C.R.A.P/)
